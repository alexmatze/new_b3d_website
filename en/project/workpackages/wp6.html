WP 6: Science transfer and industry connection



In order to establish a link between research and teaching and industrial applications and to optimize the training of outstanding young talent for potential employers in NRW, we will organize "Industry Days" and establish "Industry Forums" in WP 6. The organization of internships for students in partner companies is also planned. To achieve our goals, we are continuously looking for industry partners who will join us in facing the challenges of big data. Support us with your expertise and feel free to contact us.

In addition, WP 6 is responsible for the transfer of science to the general public and the political arena - by means of social media, lectures, press releases, etc. - with the involvement of various media.
