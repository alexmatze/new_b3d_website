WP 5: Development Drivers Data Science



WP 5 exploits the astronomical data provided by WP4 as raw material for research, development and education. We are developing common standards, defining interfaces for ML-/AI-based analyses, and designing templates for data policies, data governance frameworks, and data management plans. Another focus is visualization, for which novel methods will be developed. In addition, we are developing new approaches for the long-term archiving of radio astronomical data sets as well as a prototype description for code-to-data solutions.
